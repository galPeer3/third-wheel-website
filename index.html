What we do

Everything couples need,
in one WhatsApp chat.

🛒
Smart Shopping

Shared lists that update in real-time. Sends to your partner with one message. Learns your patterns and suggests what you're running low on.

💸
Expense Tracking

Log expenses by photo or text. Auto-categorizes everything. Weekly summaries with insights on where your money goes.

🌙
Date Night Planner

Say "plan us a date night Friday" and it handles everything — checks calendars, picks a spot, books it, sends directions to both of you.

📅
Calendar Sync

Connects to both your Google Calendars. "Are we both free Saturday?" gets an instant answer. Plans added automatically.

🧠
Couple Memory

Remembers everything — shirt sizes, anniversaries, favourite places, gift ideas. Ask anything about each other anytime.

🏠
Home Brain

Save your cleaner, electrician, plumber. Track service dates and get reminders when something's overdue.
