What is Third Wheel?

Third Wheel ("the Service") is an AI-powered couples assistant that operates through WhatsApp. It is developed and operated by Gal Peer ("we", "us", "our") as a personal project currently in beta.

Third Wheel helps couples manage daily life together — including shared shopping lists, expense tracking, calendar coordination, date night planning, home services management, and more.

Beta notice: Third Wheel is currently in beta. Features may change and the service may be interrupted at any time. We appreciate your patience and feedback.

How the Service Works

Third Wheel uses artificial intelligence (Anthropic's Claude) to understand your messages and respond helpfully. Your conversations are processed by Claude's API to generate responses.

The Service integrates with third-party services you authorize, including:

  • Google Calendar — to read and create events
  • Google Maps — to find places and directions
  • WhatsApp — as the primary communication channel
  • Ontopo — for restaurant reservations
  • OpenAI Whisper — for voice message transcription

You can revoke access to any integration at any time by contacting us.

Our Privacy Promise

Third Wheel only works if you trust us with personal information. We take that seriously.

The short version: We collect only what's needed to make Third Wheel work. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. Your private memories stay private — even from your partner.
02

What We Collect

Information you give us directly:

  • Your name and WhatsApp phone number
  • Your partner's name and WhatsApp phone number
  • Preferences you share (coffee order, sizes, dietary restrictions, etc.)
  • Shopping lists, expenses, notes, and decisions you save
  • Places and contacts you store
  • Calendar events (if you connect Google Calendar)

Information collected automatically:

  • Your messages and conversation history (stored for 24 hours for context)
  • Photos and receipts you send (processed, not stored in raw form)
  • Voice messages (transcribed by OpenAI Whisper, audio immediately discarded)
  • Location data you share voluntarily
  • Usage patterns (what features you use, when you're active)
03

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to provide and improve Third Wheel, personalize responses, connect you with your partner's shared information, and send proactive reminders.

We do not use your data to sell to advertisers, train AI models, or share with third parties beyond what's needed to run the service.

04

Private vs Shared Data

Private memories — marked as private by you — are never visible to your partner. This is enforced at the database level, not just in the UI.

Your partner CAN see: shared shopping lists, shared expense records, preferences you marked as shared (coffee order, sizes, wishlist).

Your partner CANNOT see: your private memories, your WhatsApp conversation with Third Wheel, anything you explicitly marked as private.

05

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes your messages to generate AI responses. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI (Whisper) — transcribes voice messages. Audio is discarded immediately after transcription. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — stores your data in a PostgreSQL database hosted in the EU. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Google (Calendar & Maps) — only if you connect your Google account. Read/write Calendar access, read Maps access. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Twilio / WhatsApp — messaging infrastructure. Your messages pass through their servers.
  • Railway — hosts our server infrastructure.
06

Data Security

  • All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Google OAuth tokens encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM
  • Database access restricted — no anonymous access permitted
  • Twilio webhook requests validated using HMAC signatures
  • We never store payment card information

No system is 100% secure. In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify you promptly via WhatsApp.

07

Data Retention

  • Conversation history — kept for 24 hours, then deleted automatically
  • Shopping lists, expenses, memories — kept until you delete your account
  • Receipt images — processed and immediately discarded. Only extracted data is stored
  • Voice messages — audio discarded immediately after transcription
  • OAuth tokens — kept until you revoke access or delete your account
